Iceland

Not even 24 hours after tying the knot, Ashley and I packed our suitcases, drove to Orlando and caught a plane to a not so remote destination. Not making the best of decisions, we decided to eat Burger King right before our 7 hour flight through the night. Only getting 3 or 4 hours of sleep because

(a) We were still anxious and excited about the whole marriage thing

(b) Iceland

(c) Sleeping on a plane is not the easiest of things

Nervertheless, we finally made it, and I couldn't resist taking in the freshest, cleanest air I had ever breathed. Not wanting to hog all the air, I told Ashley to take a deep breath herself. The moment she took that unforgettable breath in, the nice diesel truck that dropped us off at the terminal chugged away. In went the diesel exhaust, and out came the Burger King. Luckily, the only person that saw what happened was an air traffic control guy who looked at us in disbelief. All he could do was shake his head, and all I could do was wait for him to say "Welcome to Iceland".
